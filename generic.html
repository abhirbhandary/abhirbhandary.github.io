As a data analyst, I possess 1 year of experience which I gained after my bachelor's degree. I had an opportunity to learn further and serve my nation for 10 years as a teacher in an engineering college.

I hold a Doctor of Computer Science & Engineering degree, which is a testament to my dedication to academic excellence. During my work as a teacher, I taught computer and information science-related subjects. I used to take classes every day for students and have organized and given various workshops which gave me valuable experience in leadership and organization. I have prepared notes and assignments for students and published papers on my research in various reputable international journals.

Answer to the question - 'Why Career Change?'

“Life begins at the end of your comfort zone.” I had completed all my aspirations in teaching and reached the highest qualification in my profession. My wife had moved to Germany and started working. I decided to take up a new challenge in life. Data for good is always something that was something very close to me. The best place to start was from the beginning.

In addition to my professional aspirations, I was a mentor for a student group called 'VISTA'. I also administer a Facebook group called 'Indian family reunion visa for Germany' with more than 1000 members. The group provides members with all information related to spouse visas and helps connect people from various places. I believe it is my way to give back to the community and help people.

My diverse experiences have taught me resilience, perseverance, and a strong work ethic, which I am excited to apply to my career as a data analyst. I am eager to continue learning, growing, and contributing my skills to the field.
